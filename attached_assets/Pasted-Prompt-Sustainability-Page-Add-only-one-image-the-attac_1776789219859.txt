FAQs Content (English only):

When was tabanni founded?
tabanni was registered as an NPO in April 2021 under The Jordanian Society for Animal Protection (JSAP). It has been active since 2019.

Who is behind tabanni?
tabanni is led by a team of volunteers and animal advocates who were active in animal welfare before officially registering the organization.

What is tabanni’s mission?
tabanni promotes animal welfare, awareness, and education, focusing on rescue efforts and Trap-Neuter-Return (TNR) programs to control stray populations humanely.

Does tabanni have a shelter?
No, tabanni does not operate a shelter but supports individual rescuers and partner organizations in rehoming rescued animals.

Does tabanni accept donations?
Yes, tabanni relies entirely on donations from supporters. Contributions cover medical care, food, boarding, and rescue operations.

Scroll below to donate!

How can I support tabanni?
You can donate through bank transfer or in-person pickup. We also welcome volunteers and foster families to temporarily care for rescued pets.

Scroll below to donate or foster.

How are donations used?
100% of donations go towards medical treatment, boarding, food, transportation, rehabilitation, and essential supplies.

Why does tabanni pay for boarding?
Finding adopters and fosters is challenging. When no options are available, boarding is the only alternative to ensure animals remain safe until adoption.

Does tabanni help with reported rescues?
Yes, we guide and support individuals who report animal rescues, encouraging community members to take action.

Are all adoption posts tabanni’s rescues?
No, some adoption posts are from external rescuers, and we help by promoting them to increase their chances of finding homes.

Can I post my pet for adoption on tabanni’s platform?
Yes, fill out our intake form, and we will share your pet’s details as soon as possible.

Does tabanni receive international funding?
No, tabanni does not receive international funding and operates solely through local donations.

Does tabanni help with lost pets?
Yes! We have a dedicated Instagram page for lost and found pets to help reunite them with their families.

What are tabanni’s values?
tabanni believes that animals deserve a dignified life and should not be treated as commercial products. We oppose illegal pet trade and advocate for ethical treatment.
